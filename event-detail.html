About the event

The North Africa AI Builders Summit is a premier regional gathering designed for AI engineers, system architects, and technology innovators shaping the next generation of intelligent systems.

The summit focuses on the rapidly evolving ecosystem of generative AI, large language models, and scalable AI infrastructure. Experts will explore how modern AI platforms are transforming software development, automation, and enterprise systems.

Participants will gain insight into real-world AI implementations, modern model deployment strategies, and best practices for building reliable and scalable AI-powered applications.

What to expect

  • Keynotes from leading AI engineers and architects
  • Technical sessions on generative AI systems
  • Deep dives into scalable AI infrastructure
  • Workshops on deploying real-world AI applications
  • Discussions on AI architecture and platform design
  • Networking with the North African AI community
