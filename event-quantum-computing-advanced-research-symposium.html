Quantum Computing & Advanced Research Symposium

In person

A research-focused symposium exploring quantum computing breakthroughs, advanced algorithms, and next-generation computational research.

Register now 📅 Add to calendar

About the event

The Quantum Computing & Advanced Research Symposium is an academic-grade event bringing together researchers, scientists, and computational theorists to explore the frontier of quantum technologies and their implications for artificial intelligence.

The symposium covers quantum algorithms, error correction, quantum hardware developments, and the emerging intersection of quantum computing with machine learning — shedding light on both near-term and long-term quantum advantage scenarios.

Attendees will engage with cutting-edge research presentations, collaborative workshops, and cross-disciplinary discussions that bridge theoretical quantum science with practical computational applications.

What to expect

  • Keynote lectures from leading quantum physicists and computer scientists
  • Research paper presentations on quantum algorithms
  • Sessions on quantum hardware and error correction techniques
  • Workshops on quantum-classical hybrid computing models
  • Panel discussions on the future timeline of quantum advantage
  • Collaborative networking with the global quantum research community
